Hospital Management System (designing)

A complete solution for managing hospital operations in Odoo

Overview

The Hospital Management System module is designed to help hospitals manage their operations seamlessly. This module integrates various components like patient management, visit scheduling, disease tracking, and diagnosis recording into one centralized system. It is built on the Odoo platform and offers an intuitive interface for both administrative and medical staff.

Features

  • Patient Management - Register and manage patient details such as name, birth date, emergency contact, and doctor.
  • Visit Scheduling - Schedule visits for patients with doctors and assign diseases to the visits.
  • Disease Management - Manage diseases with parent-child relationships and categorize them.
  • Diagnosis Recording - Link diagnoses to visits and track the approval process, especially for interns.
  • Doctor and Staff Management - Manage doctors and their assignments, ensuring no double-booking for patients.
  • Patient Visit History - View and manage all visits made by a patient, along with their corresponding diagnoses.

Installation

To install the module, follow these steps:

  • Download the module from the repository.
  • Place it in your Odoo custom add-ons directory.
  • Update the Odoo app list and install the module via the Odoo interface.

Dependencies

  • Odoo 14.0 or later
  • Python 3.x
  • PostgreSQL
